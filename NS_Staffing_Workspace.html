How pricing works here

Rate = Cost ÷ (1 − Margin). We share cost internally so the team can choose a price; we share the resulting rate card (price) with the client to convey scope. The Quote Builder does both from one screen — flip the Internal / Client toggle.

Anchors: generic L1–L3 land $3,000–$6,000/mo; PM ~$8,000. Niche skills carry a modest premium (within reason).

Principles we work to

  • Quality of experience over years. Pressure-test AMS/agentic claims.
  • Triage every profile green / amber / pass before it goes to a client.
  • Flag resume inflation, name/location/education gaps, and CV-factory bullet patterns.
  • Format B keeps the client name confidential; anonymized profiles remove only the candidate's name and keep real org names.
  • FTE mandates need FTE-capable agencies — contract-heavy vendors are weak fits.
